Anonymous Analytics

EyeRest uses Aptabase, a privacy-first analytics service, to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand which features are used and improve the app.

What is tracked (anonymously):

  • App launches (platform: Mac or iPhone)
  • Timer actions (start, pause, resume)
  • Break completions and skips
  • Achievement unlocks
  • Settings changes (which setting, not personal data)

Aptabase does not use cookies, does not track across apps, and does not collect IP addresses, device IDs, or any personal information. Data is processed in the EU and is fully GDPR compliant.

iCloud Sync

If you use multiple Apple devices, EyeRest can sync your settings via iCloud (NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore). This is handled entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure. We do not operate any servers and have no access to your synced data.

HealthKit (iOS)

On iPhone, EyeRest can optionally write mindfulness minutes to Apple Health. This requires your explicit permission. Health data is stored locally by Apple's HealthKit framework and is never sent to us or any third party.

Calendar Access

EyeRest can optionally access your calendar to skip breaks during meetings (Pro feature). Calendar data is read locally using Apple's EventKit and is never transmitted anywhere. This requires your explicit permission.

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store any payment information.
